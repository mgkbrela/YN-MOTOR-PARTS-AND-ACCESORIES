About Us

YN Motorwheels Parts and Accessories is based in Tagbilaran city, Bohol. It is a retailer of motor parts, accessories, and other related services. This company is independent from the entirety of the Huan family business and founded on April 2015 by Adriano Huan and has since relocated to a new location in the beginning of 2022, having previously been located at a relatively smaller location. The company consists of 30 or so employees, and a single store location.

Company Vision

To be the Best Motor Parts Retailer in Bohol.

Company Mission

To provide the Best Customer Service to our Customers.
